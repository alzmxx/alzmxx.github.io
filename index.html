I currently hold the position of Research Professor of Transportation Engineering at Tongji University. Prior to joining Tongji, I served as a Postdoctoral Visiting Fellow at the Harvard Kennedy School. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Civil and Urban Engineering at the New York University, as well as obtaining a BSc degree from Jilin University and an MSc degree from Tsinghua University. My research has been dedicated to developing analytical and computational algorithms to improve traffic efficiency and resilience in increasingly connected cities.
